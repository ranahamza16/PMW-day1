# 3D Reconstruction Methods Comparison

This document provides a comparative analysis of five core 3D reconstruction methods, evaluated for their suitability in the PreserveMy.World (PMW) digital preservation platform.

| Method | What it does | Input Requirements | Output Type | Hardware Needs | Difficulty | Time to Run | Best for PMW | Beginner Experiment |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **COLMAP** (Photogrammetry) | Reconstructs 3D models by finding matching points across multiple overlapping images. | 50-500+ overlapping images (.jpg, .png), camera intrinsics (optional). | Point clouds, textured 3D meshes (OBJ, PLY). | High CPU/GPU, 16GB+ RAM. | Intermediate | 1-4 hours | Accurate, textured archiving of physical artifacts. | [COLMAP Colab](https://colab.research.google.com/github/cvg/colmap/blob/main/doc/colmap.ipynb) |
| **NeRF** (Neural Radiance Fields) | Trains a neural network to memorize a scene and synthesize novel, photorealistic views. | 50-200 images, camera poses (often extracted via COLMAP). | View synthesis (video), implicit 3D volumes. | High-end GPU (VRAM 12GB+). | Advanced | 1-10 hours | Highly reflective/transparent objects, photorealistic web viewers. | [Nerfstudio](https://docs.nerf.studio/) setup |
| **Gaussian Splatting** | Represents scenes as millions of 3D Gaussian blobs, enabling real-time photorealistic rendering. | Images + camera poses (via COLMAP), sparse point cloud. | .ply file of Gaussian splats. | Modern GPU (8GB+ VRAM). | Advanced | 15 mins - 1 hour | Fast web-based rendering for immersive digital exhibits. | [Luma AI Web/App](https://lumalabs.ai/) (No-code option) |
| **Monocular Depth Estimation** | Uses deep learning to predict the distance of every pixel from a single 2D image. | 1 single image. | 2D depth map (grayscale image), pseudo-3D point cloud. | Moderate CPU or low-end GPU. | Beginner | < 1 minute | Quick 2.5D visual effects, parallax, and depth-aware archiving. | Hugging Face Spaces (MiDaS) or local script |
| **Structure-from-Motion (SfM)** | Estimates 3D structures from 2D image sequences that may lack overlapping matching features found in dense photogrammetry. | Video or image sequences. | Sparse point clouds, camera trajectories. | Moderate CPU/GPU. | Intermediate | 10 mins - 1 hour | Reconstructing scenes from archival video footage. | Meshroom (AliceVision SfM pipeline) |
